## Regional Islamic Variations

### Different Terms for Same Concepts
- Prayer: Salah (Arabic), Namaz (Persian/Urdu), Ibadah (General)
- Mosque: Masjid (Arabic), Jamia (Arabic), Mandir (Not Islamic - Hindu)
- God: Allah (Arabic), Ilah (Arabic), Rabb (Lord)

## Negative Keywords to Avoid

Keywords to avoid when targeting Islamic content to prevent inappropriate associations:
- Extremism
- Radicalization
- Political Islam (unless specifically intended)
- Anti-religious content
- Misrepresented Islamic concepts
